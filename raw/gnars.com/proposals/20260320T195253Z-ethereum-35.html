6 years ago a duo of brothers expanded their dad 80´s cave diving brand,

(Nohoch = Giant in Mayan,) into a surf school by hiring educators that provides mentorship in skateboarding, parkour, stand up paddle board, and archery, to 100 kids per week.

*Through collaboration with renowned athletes, Nohoch organizes summer camps and sports trips all around Mexico to promote cultural sport competition in collaboration with local communities; reaching a total of 60 events just last year 2023!
