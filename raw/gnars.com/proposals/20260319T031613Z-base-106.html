Description

1. BUILDING GNARLY

Sponsorship and funding for creative projects that promote DHNOUNS in Latin America and Colombia, through the brush and skateboard of DCOT.

Sports sponsorship and the creation of physical and digital products for the community, both in-person (IRL) and online, along with the creation of content for basic education in Web3 and DAOs.

2. How will I do it?

DCOT proposes traveling to two competitions — one in Colombia and another in El Salvador — creating four infographics and promotional material, editing videos, painting a mural in Bogotá (Guadalupe), and connecting people in the IRL sports space with the “gnar-vision” of DHNOUNS.

The goal is to link sports with the open commerce of the Internet, merging extreme sports with technology and building the vision of DHNOUNS and GNARS DAO to consolidate a strong community around both worlds: sports and cryptocurrencies.

  • Dissemination of illustrated material with GNARS logos, explaining DCOT’s art and what GNARS DAO is.

What DCOT will do for GNARS

DCOT will focus on painting, skating, and developing the proposal described in this project.
GNARS will receive content that generates creative value on-chain, building trajectory and community around this content.

Additionally, DCOT will connect with more athletes, teach about accelerating sports through blockchain and DAO collaboration, share real experiences, and involve more athletes.

DCOT will act as the deployer of the DHNOUNS token contracts, manage the deployer wallet, and administer the website dhnouns.wtf.

6. How we organize

Vision with GNARS:
Building together in speed sports, connecting the professional sports world with the artistic and tech community.

Performance:
With this investment, DCOT aims to be a bridge between art and downhill, building the DAO vision and empowering athletes through blockchain technology.

Long-term vision:
Attracting a strong user base to the crypto ecosystem through illustrated and educational content.
Monetization profits will fund IRL on-chain events, strengthening the DAO community.

Objective:
Establish a decentralized and autonomous organization across different countries, merging sports and technology for the present and future generations of downhill athletes.

7. Final Comments

This project maintains a creative, athletic, and community-driven style. It integrates downhill sports, art, blockchain technology, DAO community building, and crypto content — empowering GNARS and incorporating more athletes into the Base ecosystem and community.
