Public Goods

Green Plan

The idea is to invest 1000 USD in the reforestation of an area that was deforested due to a fire. This area is Rebio (biological reserve) de Comboios, a surf spot, rich in culture and biodiversity in Brazil, which suffered the biggest environmental crime in the history of Brazil, with the dumping of 40 million m3 of toxic mud in the Rio Doce flowing into the mouth of the river, right next to this fire region.

The base of the Tamar Project in Espírito Santo is also located in the reserve, carrying out various actions, where professionals in the area monitor the beaches and local biome.


Aerial view of the area that was devastated by the fire

Map created for this proposal by Tunan Thome, tuberider oceanographer to help understand the area, where within the green area is REBIO and the red line is the place of fire & reforestation and the red diamond corresponds to the mouth of the Doce River.

This project will be an honor to carry out and be able to share with all you. It's already something that I really enjoy and have been doing for a few years with local regency friends, but for personal use, now being able to help the community is incredible.
