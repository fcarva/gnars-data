Description

This droposal aims to spread the culture and share 80% of the mint with the Gnars community.

The film follows the journey of Garcia Rodrigues & Renan Carvalho in Uganda, where, together with the “Skate Transforms Lives” project and partner brands, they distributed new skateboarding equipment to six communities, each with its own DIY project. During this experience, they immersed themselves in new cultures and helped break social barriers through skateboarding.
The documentary aims to present the perspective of local skateboarders, sharing their stories and showcasing how skateboarding has positively impacted their lives. This work reinforces Gnars' community-driven principles, spreading a message of unity and proving that, even with limited resources, significant change is possible.
