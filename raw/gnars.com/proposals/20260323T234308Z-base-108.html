SKATE ACROSS AFRICA (EAST COAST - SOUTH COAST)

TL;DR

We (Isaac + Ephraim from Uganda, together with Jaayfilms) are embarking on a Skate Across Africa Mission in February 2026. Starting in Uganda and skating through East & Southern Africa (Uganda → Rwanda → Tanzania → Zambia → Botswana → South Africa), we will ride on Gnars/Nouns customized skateboards the entire journey. Along the way, we’ll onboard new skaters and communities, create powerful content, and showcase Gnars as the backbone of Africa’s skateboarding future and web3 technology.

Together with Gnars from the very first push in Uganda, this Mission continues across the following African nations, each one (FLAG) representing a step forward uniting the continent, connecting shredders, communities, across every border we cross.

HISTORY OF ISAAC AND EPHRAIM (African Skaters)

Isaac

Co-founder & Executive Director of Kampala Skateboard Initiative (KSI), Vision, Strategy, International Relations, Organizer of workshops, Gnars ambassador in Uganda,Africa previously supported by Gnars with boards, gear, and funds.

Ephraim

Co-founder & Creative Director of KSI, Skater, Filmer, and Community leader; co-pilot in KSI’s push to grow skateboarding culture in Uganda.

IMPACT

We have already made a powerful impact in Uganda through the support of Gnars, onboarding skaters, building communities, and proving how skateboarding can transform youth culture. This journey across Africa is the next step: carrying that same energy into new countries and touching even more lives.
Together: Onboarded dozens of Ugandan youth into web3, ran Gnars workshops (including noggles maufacturing, skate hive and Farcaster activation), and built Gnars visibility in East Africa.

American Partner: Jaayfilms

GOAL

Skate across Africa (Uganda to South Africa) without cars or buses – fully on Gnars boards.

  • Generate high engaging content for Gnars POD Media.

  • Diversify Gnars as a streaming platform for this journey throughout.

Onboard new skaters and communities each country crossed.

  • Create a blueprint for Gnars skateparks and noggles rails across Africa.

  • Mint exclusive video NFTs from the mission for the Gnars treasury.

  • Elevate Gnars visibility through Instagram Live, Farcaster, Skate Hive.

  • The main goal of this mission is to raise momentum and funding for the construction of the Biggest Skatepark in Africa, designed by PHARRA PROFILE .This skatepark will be a landmark project, capable of hosting international skateboarding competitions and athletes from all corners of the world.
