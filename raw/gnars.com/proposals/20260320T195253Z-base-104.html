New Nounstacle inauguration in Devconnect - The cherry of the cake

TL;DR

Inaugurate a new Nounstacle installation in Argentina during Devconnect ETH and the Nouns Siders event, as part of the SKA7E & JAZZ VOL. 2 skate activation, artist connect and cultural program.

                        +

Context

A lot of Nouns vectors are converging for a massive Nounish activation at Devconnect. Gnars and Builder are teaming up for a hacker house, Lasko is hosting the Noun City at Art City Event, Humpty is leading the Nounish Creators Challenge, and Luis from 7capas www.7ctv.wtf another creator behind this Nounish experience, is organizing a Ska7e & Jazz event with Gnars.

This convergence brings together diverse builders, fostering creativity and development within the Devconnect environment. With Luis based in Argentina and connected to the local skate scene, and other partners engaging with the broader Nouns ecosystem, the event bridges culture, community, and gnars innovation.

Here is the plan, a shuttle is going to take all the creators from humpty event at 16th and bring then to the Ska7e and Jazz nounish experience where the noggles rail are going to be waiting for them, side by side with art exibithion and generics fashion show.

The best skate events generates legacy for the city and because of that the idea of creating a new nounstacle reviving the locals project has born.

This one is the VOL2 of the Ska7e & Jazz that 7capasmag www.7ctv.wtf is doing this event. Last year was a blast, and we hope to improve the event this year.

The event

The event will take place on November 16th at Ache Paraje in Palermo, Buenos Aires. This open-air, public plaza is free and accessible to everyone. The program includes a live jazz performance accompanying skaters riding the newly installed skateable sculpture, creating an immersive blend of music, movement, and art.

Additionally, there will be an art exhibition, along with DJ and VJ performances to close the evening. The event runs from 14:00 to 23:00 hrs, with an expected attendance of over 200 people. The venue also features a bar offering a full menu of food and drinks, ensuring onchain culture and social experience.


The BONUS: Nounstacle

Considering the variation of Nounstacles and the fact that a nogglesrail is already present, the obstacle is a modular skateable sculpture composed of four individual pieces, allowing skaters to arrange and combine them freely. Two of the pieces form the Noggles logo, while the other two serve as canvases for artists to create a unique live painting directly on the sculpture.

The event will also feature a Best Trick session on the obstacles, with cash prizes for standout performances, along with filmmaker and photographer coverage capturing the local scene in a truly gnarly atmosphere.

Provide full coverage of the D.I.Y. production process of the new Nounstacle and report it through short-form media content and a detailed post-release event recap.
