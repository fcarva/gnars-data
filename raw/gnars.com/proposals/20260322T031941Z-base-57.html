📜 Summary

This proposal requests $3000 USD (plus a 15% Rounds.wtf fee) in SENDIT tokens from Gnars DAO to fund a Round on Rounds.wtf. The initiative will incentivize the Gnars community to share gnarly media created in 2024. The collected content will be compiled into a “Gnars 2024 Retro Video,” which will later be minted on Zora, providing an opportunity to generate additional revenue for the DAO.

🎯 Goals and Objectives

  1. Content Generation:
    Motivate the Gnars community to create and share their best media from 2024, showcasing the unique creativity and talent of the community.

  2. Engagement:
    Strengthen the sense of community through active participation in the Round and recognition of contributors.

  3. Revenue Generation:
    Mint the final "Gnars 2024 Retro Video" as an NFT on Zora, creating a potential revenue stream for Gnars DAO.

💰 Proposed Use of Funds

  • Round Rewards: $3000 USD in SENDIT tokens for distribution among contributors whose content receives the most engagement (likes and recasts) during the Round.
  • Rounds.wtf Fee: 15% of the requested amount (an additional $450 USD in SENDIT tokens) to cover platform fees.

Total Request: $3450 USD in SENDIT tokens.

⚒ Plan

  1. Round Setup:

    • A Round will be hosted on Rounds.wtf under a Gnars-affiliated Farcaster channel.
    • Curators: Every Gnars NFT holder will serve as a curator, with their likes and recasts determining the winners.
    • Filters will ensure submissions feature high-quality gnarly media created in 2024.

  2. Rules for Participation:

    • Submissions must feature content created in the year 2024.
    • If the submission is related to a Gnars proposal, participants must reference the specific proposal in their cast.
    • The media MUST BE POSTED ON ZORA AND THEN SHARED IN /GNARS FARCASTER CHANNEL
    • By participating in the Round, contributors grant the Gnars community permission to use their submissions in the final “Gnars 2024 Retro Video.”

  3. Community Engagement:

    • Promote the Round across Gnars DAO’s communication channels, encouraging members to participate.
    • Highlight top submissions throughout the Round to maintain enthusiasm.

  4. Video Production:

    • Curate top-rated submissions to compile the “Gnars 2024 Retro Video.”
    • Credit contributors prominently within the video.

  5. NFT Minting:

    • Mint the final video on Zora.
    • Share a portion of proceeds with contributors while allocating the remainder to Gnars DAO’s treasury.

✨ Impact and Benefits

  • Showcasing Talent: Elevate the creativity of the Gnars community by highlighting user-generated content.
  • Community Growth: Encourage participation from both existing members and potential new contributors.
  • Revenue Diversification: Generate additional income through the Zora NFT minting process.

📅 Timeline

  1. Round Launch: Immediate upon DAO vote approval.
  2. Round Duration: 10 days to allow focused and active participation.
  3. Video Production and NFT Minting
