🧾 Summary

This proposal requests:

  1. $79.96 USD reimbursement for Gnars DAO’s share of past IPFS usage
  2. $119.94 USD advance to support future infrastructure through December 2025

Total Requested: $199.90 USD

💾 Context

From September 2023 to June 2025, I personally paid monthly for IPFS bandwidth, pinning, and gateway services used across both Skatehive and Gnars platforms. (https://ipfs.skatehive.app)

(That is how we host gnars droposal videos, images for snapshot, images for proposals, as Gnars does not have a dedicated gateway, skatehive borrowed its own)

This is an essential service widely used by gnars community that makes or videos load as fast as it can be.

📊 Referrer Breakdown

Traffic analysis shows that:

  • 13% of total IPFS usage came from Gnars-related traffic (including gnars.com, localhost dev environments, and shared bandwidth)
  • Gnars Droposals, which are often video-heavy and multiple gigabytes in size, contribute the heaviest individual files passing through the gateway

📊 Cost Breakdown

  • Total IPFS cost (Sep 2023 – Jun 2025): $595.90 (paid by skatehive)
  • Gnars share (13%): $79.96

🧾 Advance for Future Infra (Aug – Dec 2025)

  • Avg monthly cost: $595.90 / 22 = $27.08
  • 5 months × $27.08 × 13% = ~$17.39
  • Rounded advance to support extra bandwidth & inflation: $119.94

💰 Total Request

  • Amount: $199.90 USD
