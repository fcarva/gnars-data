This video represents the expansion of Nounish culture into new territories, connecting communities through skateboarding, creativity, and decentralized action. By minting it onchain, we solidify this moment as part of the growing history of Gnars, pushing the boundaries of action sports and cultural expression.

Description

The NFT aims to further promote Gnars, Nogglesboard, and Nouns principles within the community, amplifying our message of creativity, innovation, and fun. It spreads the Noggles in a unique way, with crazy tricks and a raw, energetic expression of skateboarding/surfing culture.

History

For some time now, we've been creating promotional videos showcasing our Gnars community, as well as partners and subcultures like Lil Nouns, Nounspace, and darkNOUNs. To further consolidate this theme, Nogglesboard put together a short celebratory video featuring NounsDAO colors, ahead of the upcoming launch Fridays at The Park Season 3

This year, the Nogglesboard team is set to roll out several exciting projects, focusing on execution and updates for Proposal 752. This includes the production of asymmetrical surfboards, collaborations with artists, and art development, as well as the creation of our own interactive website. We hope you enjoy this journey with us there’s a special surprise waiting at the end!
