My proposal requests funding for the IndoGNARS 2 movie.

Indonesia is at the top of every surfer's bucket list. It's where the most perfect waves in the world are located. I am going to stay there for 2 months. Here's the project overview:

  • July 29th until August 8th - Boat trip in the Banyak islands - We will have a videomaker onboard and I am taking a GoPro 12 with me.

  • Connect with Thiago Okazuka and Alan Rangel, two gnarly videomakers that are currently in Indonesia, to record the surfing and lifestyle content.
