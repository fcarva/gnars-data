About me

I'm Bernardo Nassar, I'm 34 years old, I'm an extreme wave bodyboarder, content producer. One of those responsible for putting the state of Espírito Santo on the radar of the most extreme waves in the world.

I've been part of the community since February 2023. My first proposal was approved seven months ago. Unfortunately, my wallet was hacked, and I lost 90% of the funds. Despite this setback, I delivered part of the proposal, producing 8 vlog-style videos (averaging 10 minutes each), one retrospective film of 2024, and organizing two premiere events alongside Luiz Hadad for his film, 'IndoGnars 2.' I also received media coverage for my work.

Meetup's

There will be five Meetup events at our partner venue, We Believe Hub, which is already accessible to anyone holding at least 1 Gnar. The topics discussed during the meetups will focus on the integration of Web3 and sports, exploring how it has impacted not only my career as an athlete and content creator but also highlighting several other cases from the community. I also aim to go further by promoting education on the use of crypto technologies in daily life, encouraging their adoption by local businesses, similar to what has been implemented in Rolante, Rio Grande do Sul.

I already have ten merchants and service providers registered. For those partners with advertising TVs, I want to encourage them to play the Gnars YouTube channel with our productions, as described at the beginning of this proposal.
