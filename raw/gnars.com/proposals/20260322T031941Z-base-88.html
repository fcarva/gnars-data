Description

TL;DR

This Droposal (a proposal to mint Gnars media as canonical DAO NFTs) tells the story of Noggelstacles around the world, centering on the creation of the new Noggles Delta sculpture by Pharra XV in Rio de Janeiro.

80% of all mints are split with the Gnars community.

About

This film documents the making of Noggles Delta, a new sculpture installed in downtown Rio de Janeiro. It captures the entire production process while offering a brief history of Nogglestacles—from the original Noggles Rail to Gnars' involvement in ILoveXV 2022, and the spread of these nounish obstacles across the globe.

Directed by Yan Felipe, the documentary follows artist PHARRA XV over several months, chronicling the design and creation of this latest Nogglestacle. It also uncovers the roots of the movement in Rio, showing how it helped transform the city into a global node of nounish culture.

Watch, support, and share this story—an evolving chapter in the Gnars universe, grounded in the streets of Rio and shaped by the local subculture.
