Description

First NOGGLES RAIL at Medellín Colombia
Proposal

Fuzzyface.eth is a visual artist and promoter of DHNouns and Gnars in the sports community of Colombia. As a Gnar builder and proponent of decentralization ideas cc0 and Nouns, Fuzzyface.eth proposes the construction of a rail in the shape of noggles, using the design, materials, and measurements of the skate rail noggles sculpture created by Colectivo XV, incorporating one more rail on the map, this time in Medellín, Colombia Skate plaza ancianos .

How Do I Plan to Do It?
Fuzzyface contacted the organizers of the event MEDELLIN SKATEMARKET, Chonetos. The event, which will take place on August 16, 17, 18 of 2024, in the city of Medellín, the event will focus to create spaces for local brands and provide an experience that brings together different actors from the national skate scene. It opens opportunities for brands to network and create spaces for on boarding and communication.

The event of 17 of august includes a day of fair where brands that pay for their space will have a stand to offer their products, and a skate jam where the best tricks will be awarded. This will be the perfect moment to present the Noggles rail to the community, where we will donate $300 USD to the prize pool. Fuzzyface will ensure high-quality documentation of the event and experience.

Activities Proposal
Gnars will participate in two activities:

  1. Exhibitors Fair:
    17 of august 2024 at confama video reference
    We will have a booth presenting the noggles rail and inviting the community to participate in an online event where we will deposit 0.1 ETH at contest pool.
    Conduct a 25-minute onboarding talk to introduce the Medellín skater community to Gnars and Nouns.
    Noggles rail will be featured in the skate jam, awarding cash for creative tricks.
    Skate Jam:

@tomchoneto will be in charge of the construction of the railway in Medellin will handle the construction and transportation of the rail in Medellín.

fuzzyface will travel to Medellín on August 8 to oversee the construction, install Gnars logos, QR codes, and information about the artwork, and supervise the rail's paint and finishing details.

Fuzzyface.eth will also present Gnars at the stand during the event, onboarding and talking about the DAO with attendees, distributing stickers, and inviting people to join the Discord or WhatsApp community and the Gnars DAO space.

  1. After the event, we will install the noggles rail in the public space "El Puente de Colombia con San Juan (SKATE PLAZA ANCIANOS)" where the local and international skate community can visit it. We will donate this sculpture to the Medellín skate community, creating a connection point for the ONLINE EVENT that we will promote, leaving a landmark of Gnars' presence in the local skate scene.

How to Reward the Local Community
We plan to deposit 0.1 ETH in a prize pool on the platform thatsgnar.ly/dhnouns/contests , which will be distributed among the top 10 most voted videos. The winners will be chosen by the downhill nouns community. We will promote the contest as follows:

DIGITAL MONEY FOR TRICKS: Send a video doing a trick on the noggles rail and win ETH. Join a community that empowers athletes.

Noggles rail reference:

The rail noggles will be installed in the public space between August 19 and 21 where people will be able to record their trick and compete for a portion of Contest pool, this pool is open to donations where donors will get votes back to participate in noggles contest Medellin 2024.
