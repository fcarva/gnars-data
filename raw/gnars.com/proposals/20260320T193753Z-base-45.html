Description

TL;DR

Develop and maintain a dynamic website that consolidates functionalities from platforms like propdates.wtf, nouns.camp, and thatsgnar.ly, but tailored specifically for the Gnars community. This platform will unify governance, social engagement, and creative content in a single, accessible location.

Introduction

The proposers aim to create a platform that provides new and experienced users with a complete Gnars experience. This site will streamline opportunities for bidding and minting, enhance DAO transparency and communication, and integrate platforms like Propdates and Paragraph/Farcaster to keep the community informed and engaged.

🔗 New Gnars Preview

Why Another Website?

While nouns.wtf serves the core needs of the Nouns community, it remains intentionally simple to support future forks. However, websites like nouns.camp, nouns.gg, nouns.world, and propdates.wtf have evolved beyond basic functionalities, forming an unbreakable ecosystem that caters to a broader audience. Our vision is to create a permissionless platform that complements the flagship gnars.wtf without restricting innovation. This new website will prioritize community-driven features and offer a flexible environment to test new ideas.

🏠 Homepage Design

The homepage will feature key revenue streams for Gnars: Thatsgnarly NFTs (with a 20% share allocated to the Gnars treasury) and the Gnars Auction. Combining these revenue streams on a single page offers diversified contribution options for supporters with varied interests.

  • Auction Section: Encourages bidding on Gnars NFTs, potentially bundled with a commemorative Zora/Thatsgnarly NFT for added incentive.
  • Proposal Highlights: Displays a preview of current proposals. If no active proposals exist, trending Propdates will be shown.
  • Community Updates: Includes Propdates and newsletters to keep users informed.

Possible Future Enhancements

  3. Cross-Posting on Warpcast and Hive: Seamless integration for sharing content across these platforms, amplifying community reach and engagement.
  4. Governance-Based Noggle Rewards: Allow community members to claim exclusive Noggles for participating in governance actions.
