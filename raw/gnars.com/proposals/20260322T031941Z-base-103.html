Nounstacles in Venezuela🇻🇪🛹⌐◨-◨

Build the first Nounstacles in Venezuela 🇻🇪🛹⌐◨-◨ and bring Nouns Skate culture with Gnars.

Vida Verde Take the first steps towards the proliferation of Gnars with the construction and installation of the first Nountacles in Caracas, Venezuela.

📍The site:

We saw this first installation in El Hatillo, a tourist municipality where extreme sports are part of the environment. El Hatillo is located on the outskirts of Caracas, and the main sports practiced in the area include skateboarding, downhill bike, downhill skateboarding, motocross, rollerblading, and paragliding, among others.

We start at home: Our headquarters are in El Hatillo, so we want to start at home. We are currently building Vida Verde spaces using sustainable architecture with shipping containers. We have a 1,000-square-meter recreation area with a camping area, fire pit, outdoor kitchen, recreation area, vegetable garden, and outdoor classrooms, where we have trained more than 350 children, young people, and members of the local community.

We want to install Nounstacles at one of the three points marked as tourist attractions.

What is the difference between the Blue Circle and the Yellow Circle?
🔵Boulevard Sucre El Hatillo: these spaces are also used for cultural and musical events and fairs.
🟡El Hatillo Sports Route, this route marked on the map is the most used by sports enthusiasts, both for walking and cycling. The two yellow dots are frequently used stops, one is the La Lagunita straight and the other is El Hatillo Park, which was inaugurated a few months ago.

✳️ Objectives

  • Build the first Nounstacles in Venezuela 🇻🇪🛹⌐◨-◨ and bring Nouns Skate culture with Gnars.
  • Raise awareness of DAO Gnars and Nouns through the installation of the first Nounstacles in Venezuela.
  • Engage local crews with the global Gnars movement.
  • Bring new members to Gnars and Nouns through promotion and dissemination of the installation on social media.
