About

This film documents the Gnars skate tour in Argentina, capturing the full energy and atmosphere of the trip. It reflects both IRL and onchain vibes, lived intensely inside a skate hacker house, where culture, connection, and experimentation came together over nearly two weeks.

Directed by Yan Felipe, the Gnargentina Tour follows the Gnars DAO crew through an immersive journey: five days at Devconnect, building meaningful connections across the onchain ecosystem, followed by ten days dedicated to skating, exploring the city, and absorbing the local street culture.

Watch, support, and share this story—an evolving chapter in the Gnars universe, rooted in the streets of Rio and reshaped through the rhythms, textures, and subcultures of Argentina.
