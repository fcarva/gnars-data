TL;DR
We are requesting from Gnars Dao to fund the creation of DIY parkour equipment for Katwe parkour culture in Uganda. With $1,200, we will build safe, mobile, and durable structures to empower youth, grow the local movement, and represent Gnars through bold creativity and action.

OUR BACKGROUND
Katwe parkour culture (KPC) is a community-led founded by Raki in kampala, Uganda. We use parkour to inspire, uplift, and give youth a positive outlet through movement. In areas like Katwe, there are no formal parkour spaces- yet young athletes train daily in rough conditions, proving their passion and resilence.

Our goal is to provide Safe and creative training setups built from local materials. By doing this ourselves (DIY-style),including the Gnars Dao logo, we reflect both philosophy and the spirit of community innovation.

The Problem We facing

No formal training spaces or safe equipment.
High risk of injury from undafe terrian.
Limited public awareness of parkour in Uganda.
high cost of imported gear.
What we propose,
we plan to build a DIYParkour equipments including;

Vault boxes
Adjustable swing bars

balance beams
All equipment will be modular and mobile, built with professional capenters and welders, and transported to training grounds and outreach events.
