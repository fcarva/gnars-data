Description

This Droposal aims to mint a video from a surf session in Playa Hermosa, Costa Rica, featuring surfer and artist zimardrp (aka me) testing the Sweet Amnesia 5'5 fish tail in a Nogglesboard shape, while wearing a collab shirt by ARDRP x Gnars with a pancake inspired design from that morning’s breakfast.

The session lasted about two hours during our surf trip. On the way up from Pavones to the north, the swell dropped a bit, giving me the chance to try out this Nogglesboard alongside local pro Carlos Muñoz and other surfers. The board proved to deliver high speed and smooth turns.

The video for this Droposal is a simplified cut of that surf session. However, the recorded material also includes additional footage that will be part of the upcoming Nogglesboard documentary, featuring artistic shots of the board, the session itself, and Tony Bear, an Australian shaper who has been living and shaping remotely in Costa Rica for over eight years.

For this mint, we also added a split contract using a new Droposals feature powered by app.splits, directing 50% to the Gnars treasury and 50% to me (zima).
