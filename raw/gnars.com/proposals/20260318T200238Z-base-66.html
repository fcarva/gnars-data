Intro

Am Shema Ryan, a dynamic individual who seamlessly blends passion for BMX riding with expertise as a software engineer. With over three years of active participation in the BMX community, i have honed skills at the street mastering tricks and pushing my limits. Off the ramps, i channel the same dedication and precision into coding and developing innovative software solutions.

BMX Uganda Organization

BMX Uganda is a non-profit organization founded by Ryan, and kanz dedicated to promoting BMX riding and empowering Ugandan youth, starting from age five and above. The organization provides workshops, events, and training sessions to nurture talent, encourage personal growth, and support community development.

Beyond BMX, BMX Uganda actively engages in social causes, such as the “Ride Against Malaria” initiative, in collaboration with NBS Television, the Rotary Club of Kisaasi-Kyanja, and Fun Cycling Uganda. Through these efforts, BMX Uganda has demonstrated a commitment to using sports as a vehicle for positive change.

The organization actively shares its activities on Instagram, showcasing young riders, community events, and the positive impact of BMX on Ugandan youth.

How did Ryan knew about Gnars

Ryan learned about Gnars through Ephraim and Isaac from the Kampala Skateboard Initiative. They were drawn to Gnars’ mission of supporting action sports and fostering a global network of athletes and enthusiasts. The ability to connect with like-minded individuals worldwide and access new opportunities through Gnars excites them, and they are eager to contribute to the community.
