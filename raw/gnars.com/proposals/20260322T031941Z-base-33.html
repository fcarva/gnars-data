Description

Gnars fam 🤘

We (Gami and Joe Atkinson) are thrilled to announce an epic opportunity to bring the iconic Noggles Rail to the UK, with its premiere at the legendary Bay 66 Skatepark in London!

These noggles have been designed as a modular structure, making it easy to transport and set up at different locations, ensuring it can be stored at Bay 66 and readily available for future events and sessions.

The Noggles Rail has been built and will be transported from Yorkshire to London on the 24th of August, just in time for the Capital Rollers LDN JAM. This event will be a game-changer as we’ll be hosting a dedicated contest on the Noggles Rail with a £1000 prize up for grabs for quad skaters and bladers.

To top things off, we're encouraging skaters to get onchain by creating a Warpcast profile and uploading their best clips from the contest to the /gnars channel. Not only will they have the chance to win the main prize, but additional prizes will be available for those who showcase their skills on the platform.

This is an opportunity to bring the Gnars spirit to London and elevate the local skate scene, Let's make history at Bay 66! Cheers to shredding the Noggles Rail in the heart of London!
