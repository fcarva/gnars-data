Gnars Sunset IRL Docuseries Premiere Event | Open Sky Cinema

Continue prop 512 with Gnars for a great impact and onboarding big names from surf industry.

TLDR: Rickyday (me) and Sunny Pires, along with our team, passed a prop on NOUNS, to make a Docuseries in Nicarágua to conquer a Volcano in the name of Nouns as a surf adventure.

We aim to engage the Gnars community in the project. Our initial idea is to have Gnars featured in the episodes (is gonna be there). However, we would like to make an IRL event to launch the docuseries as an extra distribuition, impact and onboarding from our initial prop to Nouns.

We believe this docuseries and Gnars collaboration will significantly benefit our community.

With Prop 512 we are already showing the world what is Nouns and Gnars.

We are from Brazil and there is people from San Diego,CA talking about us. See the news here.

Rickyday and Sunny went to Nico's Podcast, a studio fully branded with noggles and gnars, was 1hrs talk about Surf, Gnars, Nouns and web3.

Everyone is invited, entry is FREE!

We would love to host an Gnarly IRL Docuseries premiere event before we launch the episodes on Flamboiar's channel. It would be a special VIP episodes for everyone attending.

We talked to Casa Somar, a renowned local guest house with a beachfront view in Búzios (Sunny's hometown). Is the perfect place, they have the shredder vibe giving kite surf, foil and wing foil classes. Casa Somar loved the idea of a local professional surfer launching his documentary there in the format of an open sky cinema.

They have a gastrobar to provide excelent food and drinks.

Combined with the press relations from prop 512 reaching out to other medias to promote it, this event is going to be massive.

The docuseries will serve as an onboarding pathway, but it's not limited to just that. We've developed a strategic awareness plan involving individuals who will amplify its impact through their media channels.

Prominent figures in the surfing world reside in Búzios and are familiar with Sunny and his family. We also aim to hold the event on July 6th, a weekend following the world surfing championship in Saquarema (WSL CT), which is near Búzios. The top professional world champions will be in the area.

We'll be sending invitation kits to surf influencers, inviting them to join us for a drink. Each kit will include an invitation card along with the following items, which have our own personalized logo for this project.

  • Truck hat;
  • T-shirt;
  • Ecobag;
  • Recycle water bottle made from bambu fiber;
  • Gnars invitation card;

Invitation card

The following kit will be sent to some influencers, as:
Familia Surf Dog (already familiarized with gnars through Nico), Gabriel Pastori, Trekinho, Imbica, Phill Rajzman, Gabriel Medina's family (Sunny is close of his family) and others.

To spark more interest, we'll be offering a complimentary welcome drink to the first 50 guests.

Giveaways:

  • Surfboard Giveaway - Brand new Full Carbon Surfboard (Sunny Pro model) from Hennek (wetworks) featuring Gnars logo! The lucky winner will receive a voucher to make the perfect surfboard with Hennek.

  • 1hr of Wakefoil/Wakeboard Class Giveaway, a gift from Casa Somar, for the lucky winner plus 2 friends.

  • 20 merch kits for Giveaway (T-shirt/hat/water bottle and ecobag).

Additionally, Flamboiar, our media partner from prop 512, will launch their magazine with Sunny's articles at this event. Free surf magazines will be distributed!

Schedule:

July 6, 2024

  • 4pm - Dj + surf band (sunset vibe)
  • 7pm - Sunny's Premiere docuseries
  • 8pm - Giveaway 20 kits, surfboard and wake class
  • 8:45pm - Surf band + Dj

Big Screen at the garden to make an open Sky Cinema.
