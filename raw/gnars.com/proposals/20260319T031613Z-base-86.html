Revenue

We are working with Vrbs to incubate revenue generating projects. We want to figure out how to help grantees drive revenue and grow the pie for everyone.

Practically, this means building out a revnet integration, team management, and revenue integrations eg: Slice/Shopify/Droposals into Flows to start.

We plan to make these features available at no cost to Gnars grantees, to help them earn revenue for Gnars.

We also plan to split 20% of the Gnars Flows revnet (that issues the TCR token) with the Gnars DAO treasury. As the Gnars flows gain more sponsorship, more folks will curate, and the treasury will earn more.

Our first idea is to help tag posts that are mints/purchase opportunities via the API, so they can be shown easily on the gnars.com homepage. We commit to working closely with Gnars to figure out how to maximize revenue back to the treasury.

Sucess Case

We are targeting two different revenue possibilities.

Base

  1. Base Sponsors Gnars content via Flows
  2. Base Branding in Flows and Gnars Page
  3. Creators wear and use Base branding in videos and photos creations
  4. Creators creates content to generate revenue for then, for the dao, for the flow and for the sponsor if sponsor opt in for revenue split

Base is running their own Flow soon. I plan to pitch them to fund a Gnars flow after their Flow has been running for a month or two.

Shredders

  1. A shredder or group of shredders eg: KSI signs up for the accelerator features
  2. They start hiring a team across Gnars with their initial salary
  3. Launch a revnet w/split to Gnars treasury
  4. Start selling merch / mints and drive revenue into revnet
  5. Attract more buyers + grow their impact and the Gnars treasury

Extra

  • Gnars website is going to Showcase shared revenue creations
