ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

  • 7Capas is a highly regarded skateboarding magazine and platform within the local Latinamerican skate community, valued not only for its focus on the sport but also for embracing the culture and lifestyle it represents.

  • Since 2008, from Latin America to the world, 7Capas has been a skate magazine with a focus on the cultural aspects surrounding the sport. With over 50 print publications across 15 years and a long list of events, 7Capas evolves into 7c-tv on the digital front, delivering fresher information while reserving the editorial side for other types of content. Together, these elements intertwine to preserve the essence of skateboarding from this side of the street.
